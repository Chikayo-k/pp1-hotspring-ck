Explore the hotsprings of Japan

Do you want to have fun and enjoy but also relax and get rid of fatigue duaring your vacation?
Hot springs is called "Onsen" by Japanese people.

Japan is a country that is abundant with natural hot springs. There are more than 25000 hotspring throughout Japan. From these natural hotsprings there is approximately 3000 established onsen(Bath houses) that serve the public.

Benefits of Onsen

There are many benefits having hotsprings. Your blood circulation improves which means your metabolism increase. This helps to remove waste from your bodies and helps recover from physical exhustion. Onsen water contains chemical compositions that works for your helth and skin very well. Having a hotspring is also good for your mental health because your sympathetic nervous system is dormant and your body actually relax deeply.

Japanese Style Hotel

Have you decided where to stay? Staying in Japanese-style hotels is one of the biggest recommendations when you travel to Japan. Many Japanese-style hotel facilities onsen within the hotel. They normally have big outdoor baths and also small bath for each room. Staying in a Japanese-style hotel, you can experience great hospitality from the host. Have triditional japanese food in your room will make your travel experience exceptional and will be your great memories.
