Hot Spring In Japan

Looking for as way to relax and have fun while on your trip?
Why not try out the tradional bath houses of Japan or as they are known here "Onsen".

Japan is a country that is abundant with natural hot springs. There are more than 25000 hotsprings throughout the islands of Japan.
From these natural hotsprings there is approximately 3000 established onsen(Bath houses) that serve the Japanese people.
Onsen come in a wide range from the tradional outdoor baths to the new indoor baths. They can be enjoyed in tradional style Japanese inns or dedicated bath houses throughout the country.

Benefits of Onsen

The benefits of visiting a hot spring are wide and will have a different impact on everyone. Some of the main benefits are relaxation for both the body and the mind.
More specifically it promotes relaxation and healing in muscles from the heat of the hotspring. It helps improve the skin from the naturally occurring minerals that are brought up through the earth.
It improves circulation and relieves joint and inflammation pain. Works great for people with Arthritis or the elderly.
By allowing yourself to soak in the hot waters of the hot spring it can help bring about a sense of deep relaxation which can help both to reduce stress and improve mental health.

Japanese Style Hotel

Have you decided where to spend your time in Japan?
When travelling to Japan staying in a Japanese-style hotel is one of the biggest recommendations we can give. Not only do many Japanese-style hotels have onsen within their facilities but they normally have big outdoor traditional style baths as well as small private ones for their guests to enjoy. Staying in a Japanese-style hotel, you can experience traditional customs and exceptional hospitality that have been honed over generations. Explore Japanese food in your room and enjoy simple tea services which will make your travel experience one to remember.
